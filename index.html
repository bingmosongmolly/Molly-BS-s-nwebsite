Welcome to My Website

Hello and welcome! This site is a small personal space where I share who I am and what I’m currently learning. Building this website helps me practice the fundamentals—HTML for structure, CSS for style, and habits that make tiny projects feel professional: clear navigation, readable typography, and thoughtful layout. On the second page, I go deeper into a topic I enjoy and explain why it matters, how I approach learning it, and what I plan to experiment with next.

Outside of coding, I like tools that make ideas easier to see: charts, diagrams, and tidy documents. Over time I’ll add short posts, class notes, and mini projects that document what I tried. My goal isn’t perfection; it’s steady progress and sharing enough detail that someone else can follow along. Thanks for visiting, and feel free to reach out with feedback—I’m always happy to learn from others.
